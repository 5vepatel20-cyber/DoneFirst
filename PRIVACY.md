# Privacy Policy — DoneFirst

**Last updated:** July 2026

## 1. What We Collect
- **Account data**: email, display name (from parent sign-up)
- **Child data**: name, color/emoji preference, homework tasks & photos, streak count
- **Photos**: homework proof images uploaded by children

## 2. How We Use Data
- Parent-child homework accountability (sessions, locks, proof verification)
- AI proof verification via Mistral API (images are sent temporarily, not stored by Mistral)
- Streak tracking and milestone celebrations

## 3. Third-Party Services
- **Supabase** (database, auth, storage): data is stored and hosted
- **Mistral AI**: proof images forwarded for verification; Mistral does not retain data

## 4. Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)
- This app is directed at parents and their children
- Parents control all data; children's data is only accessible to the linked parent
- Parents can delete their child's data at any time via Account Deletion
- No personal data is sold or shared with advertisers
- No child data is used for profiling

## 5. Data Retention
- Data is retained until account is deleted
- Proof images are stored until parent deletes the session or account

## 6. Your Rights
- Access, correct, or delete your data at any time
- Delete account in Settings → Delete Account (cascades to all children and associated data)
